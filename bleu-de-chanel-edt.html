✦ About This Fragrance

Bleu de Chanel EDT is the original and iconic version of this legendary fragrance — a clean, sophisticated masculine scent that has become a modern classic since its 2010 launch. Created by master perfumer Jacques Polge, it captures the essence of a man who defies convention and forges his own path. The opening is a vibrant cocktail of citrus and grapefruit — fresh, clean, and instantly recognizable. The heart of dry cedar and labdanum adds a woody, slightly smoky quality that gives the fragrance its distinctive character. The base of white musk and incense creates a clean yet subtly mysterious finish. Lighter and fresher than the EDP, this version is ideal for warmer weather and daytime wear.
